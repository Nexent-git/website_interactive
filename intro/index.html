The cognitive core of the Nexent AI platform, expanded and reinforced for assessment. A library of orchestrated reasoning, generation and evaluation skills that designs scenarios, probes candidates and grades evidence with expert-level consistency.

Skills + Avatars + Systems: the engine that makes AI-native assessment possible.

The gap

Talent assessment hasn't kept up

Traditional assessment centers were designed for another era. Three structural limits hold them back.

01

It doesn't scale

Hundreds of assessor hours for a single hiring round. Every additional candidate multiplies cost and calendar time.

02

It's inconsistent

Inter-rater variability, fatigue and implicit bias mean two equally strong candidates can walk away with very different scores.

03

It uses generic content

Pre-written scenarios designed for broad applicability: the same case study for a fintech PM and a hospital director.

The TALENS solution

AI-native, end to end

The entire pipeline is driven by AI: configuration, content, assessment and evaluation. No pre-written human content required.

WHAT

You define the WHAT

Industry, role, seniority and target competencies. Your judgement about what matters stays exactly where it belongs: with you.

HOW

The AI handles the HOW

Generates tailored scenarios, runs adaptive assessments and produces evidence-based reports, at any scale, with total consistency.

Not AI bolted onto traditional assessment, but assessment reimagined from the ground up.

How it works

A four-phase, fully automated pipeline

PHASE 1

Configure

An AI assistant guides campaign setup, suggesting modules, validating coherence and ensuring full competency coverage.

PHASE 2

Generate

Scenarios, characters and dossiers generated in real time, tailored to sector, role and seniority. Never a recycled template.

PHASE 3

Assess

The AI conducts adaptive sessions: probing, injecting complications and adjusting pressure in real time as the candidate responds.

PHASE 4

Evaluate

Structured reports with per-competency ratings, narrative assessments, evidence quotes and clear recommendations.

Human involvement: ~15 minutes on Day 1. Everything else is fully automated.

What sets it apart

Unique, adaptive, evidence-based

Unique & contextual

A Senior PM in fintech and one in healthcare get different scenarios: same competency, different manifestation.

Real-time adaptation

Vague answer? It probes deeper. Exceptional depth? It escalates complexity. The assessment goes as deep as the candidate allows.

Evidence-based

Every rating anchored to direct evidence: specific quotes with contextual framing. Fully auditable and defensible.

Seniority calibration
