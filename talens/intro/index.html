Start familiar

The assessment you already know

A traditional assessment center rests on three ingredients that have proven their value for decades.

01

Realistic exercises

Candidates step into situations that resemble the real job: interviews, role plays, case studies, simulations.

02

Expert observers

Trained assessors watch how people actually behave under pressure, not just what a CV claims.

03

Structured evaluation

Behaviour is rated against defined competencies and becomes a report that supports a real decision.

TALENS keeps this exact logic. What changes is how it gets done.
E-assessment

From meeting rooms to a browser

E-assessment simply means running those same exercises online. It solves the logistics, and it is the foundation TALENS builds on: AI takes each of these gains further.

Anywhere, anytime

Candidates join from home or from the office, when it suits them. No travel, no venues, no scheduling puzzles.

Plus, with AI: no assessor needs to be on the other side, so a session can start the moment the candidate is ready.

The same process for everyone

Every candidate goes through the same steps and is measured against the same standard, from the first of the day to the hundredth.

Plus, with AI: the standard stays identical, but the content comes alive: scenarios adapt to each person's role, seniority and answers.

Everything on record

Each answer and interaction is captured, so any evaluation can be reviewed and verified later.

Plus, with AI: recordings become evidence: every rating is linked to the exact words that justify it.

Ready to scale

Reaching more candidates becomes far easier: sessions run in parallel, with no venues or travel multiplying costs and timelines.

Plus, with AI: content and evaluation are generated automatically, so effort stays flat at any scale.

Where AI comes in

What the AI actually does

Modern AI can hold a natural conversation, write realistic scenarios and evaluate answers against clear criteria. TALENS uses these abilities to cover four roles that used to require a whole team.

THE AUTHOR

Writes the scenario

A fresh, realistic case for each candidate, tuned to sector, role and seniority. Never a recycled template.

THE ACTOR

Plays the characters

Gives voice and personality to the people in the exercise: a colleague, a client, a stakeholder who reacts naturally to what the candidate says.

THE INTERVIEWER

Runs the session

Asks questions, listens, follows up and adapts the difficulty in real time, the way a skilled assessor would.

THE EVALUATOR

Drafts the report

Reads everything, rates each competency and quotes the exact moments that justify every score.

The AI prepares the evidence. The decision stays with people.
Fair questions

The questions everyone asks

Reasonable doubts deserve straight answers.

"Is it fair?"

The AI applies the same standard to everyone: it never gets tired and never has a bad day. And every score comes with quotes you can check yourself.

"Does it replace people?"

No. It removes the repetitive work: writing cases, running sessions, drafting reports. Your expertise moves to where it matters, the decision.

"Is it a black box?"

The opposite: every rating shows what was asked, what the candidate answered and why it was scored that way, with the full transcript attached.

"How do candidates take it?"

Natural conversations and exercises built around the candidate's world and role, with a light touch that puts them at ease: no panel across the table, no batteries of multiple-choice questions. And the numbers back it up: in a field experiment with 70,000 applicants, when given the choice, 78% picked the AI interviewer over the human recruiter.
